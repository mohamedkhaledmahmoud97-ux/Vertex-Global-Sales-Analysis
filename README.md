   #                                       Vertex-Global-Sales-Analysis
Vertex Global Sales Analytics: An executive dashboard monitoring $85.26M in revenue across global sectors and regions.
📊 Case Study: Vertex Global Sales Performance Analysis
By: Mohamed Khaled Mahmoud Ibrahim

1. Executive Summary

This project analyzes the global sales performance of Vertex Global, covering a total revenue of $85.26 Million. The goal was to identify growth drivers, evaluate regional performance, and optimize the sales force effectiveness to maximize profitability.

3. Business Task
   
To provide executive leadership with actionable insights regarding:
Which business sectors drive the highest revenue?
Which geographic regions are overperforming vs. underperforming?
Which products and sales agents are the primary contributors to the bottom line?

4. Data Process (The Approach)

Prepare: Cleaned and structured a massive dataset of international sales transactions.
Process: Used Advanced Excel (Power Query & Pivot) to handle data integrity, currency normalization, and relational modeling.
Analyze: Conducted multi-dimensional analysis including Waterfall charts for product contribution and Pareto analysis for sales agents.
Visualize: Designed a high-impact, interactive executive dashboard for real-time KPI monitoring.

5. Key Findings (The Insights)
   
Sector Dominance: The Retail ($4.8M) and Medical ($3.2M) sectors are the strongest verticals, accounting for a significant portion of the growth.
"This project demonstrates my ability to bridge the gap between complex data processing and executive-level business strategy, a core skill gained during the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate."
Regional Performance: The West Region ($8M) is the leading territory, while the East region shows potential for optimization.
Product Stars: Vertex Pro ($7.1M) is the flagship product with the highest market penetration.
Top Talent: A small group of elite agents (led by Darcel S.) generates over 15% of total revenue.

7. Strategic Recommendations (The Action)

Resource Reallocation: Increase marketing spend and sales headcount in the Retail and Medical sectors.
Expansion: Replicate the sales tactics used in the West Region across other territories to bridge the performance gap.
Upsell Strategy: Launch a "Premium Upgrade" campaign targeting customers of lower-tier products to move them toward the Vertex Pro line.
Retention: Implement a "High-Performer Retention Plan" for top-tier sales agents to ensure long-term stability
